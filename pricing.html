Frequently Asked Questions

Do credits expire?

No! Once you purchase credits, they remain in your account forever. The only exception is the free trial credits which expire after 7 days.

Can I get a refund?

Due to the nature of crypto payments and instant credit delivery, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. We offer a free trial so you can test the platform before purchasing.

What happens if a scrape fails?

If our system fails to extract data from a website, no credits are deducted. We only charge for successful extractions.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes! The more credits you purchase, the lower the per-credit cost. Enterprise customers can contact us for custom volume pricing.

Which cryptocurrencies do you accept?

We accept Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), USDT, USDC, BNB, and other major cryptocurrencies. Payment is processed through secure blockchain transactions.

Can I share credits with my team?

Yes! Pro and Enterprise plans include team features where credits can be shared across multiple team members from a single account.
