Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a new extraction?

Click on "New Extraction" in the sidebar, enter the target URL, select extraction type and depth level, then click "Start Extraction".

How are credits calculated?

Credits are based on extraction depth and type. Level 1 (Homepage) costs 5 credits, Level 2 costs 10 credits, and Level 3 costs 20 credits.

Can I export my data?

Yes! All extracted data can be exported to CSV format from the Email Lists and Phone Lists pages.

How do I upgrade my plan?

Go to Credits & Billing page and select the plan that fits your needs. Changes take effect immediately.
